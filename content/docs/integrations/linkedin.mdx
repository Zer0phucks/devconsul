# LinkedIn Integration

Publish professional updates to LinkedIn from your GitHub activity.

## Setup

1. **Settings > Platforms > LinkedIn**
2. Click **"Connect with LinkedIn"**
3. OAuth authorization (Read/Write permissions)
4. Choose profile or company page
5. Status: "Connected"

## Configuration

### Post Types
- Personal profile updates
- Company page posts
- Articles (long-form)

### Content Settings
- **Max Length:** 3000 characters
- **Professional Tone:** Automatically enabled
- **Include Links:** GitHub URLs
- **Media:** Images, documents, videos

## Features

**AI Optimization for LinkedIn:**
- Professional language style
- Industry-relevant hashtags
- Engagement hooks
- Call-to-action endings

**Publishing Options:**
- Post to profile
- Post to company page
- Schedule posts
- Auto-publish toggle

## Best Practices

- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts per week
- **Length:** 150-300 words optimal
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant tags
- **Timing:** Business hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
- **Engagement:** Ask questions, encourage discussion

## Rate Limits

LinkedIn API: 100 posts per day (recommend max 5/day)
